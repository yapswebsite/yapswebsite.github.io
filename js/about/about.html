About Us

Young Australian Problem Solver(YAPS) is a not-for-profit organization that aims to improve students problem-solving skills.

Young Australian Problem Solver (YAPS) establishes objectives that focus on providing professional experience to students to improve employability and set them ready for the workforce. We strive to create the best opportunities to develop students' problem-solving, teamwork, time management, presentation and research skills.

Mission

We are highly committed in delivering social impacts and significant contributions to the society by developing students’ employability skills and solving social enterprises’ toughest problems.

Vision

We collaborate students with social enterprises. We strive to create the best real-life experience for student, and expose them to real-world situations. We are here to make a difference and we believe, together with every committed individual, we can establish positive changes in the society.
