About Us

The Young Australian Problem Solver (YAPS) is an independent not-for-profit student led organisation that aims to enhance students' problem-solving skills.

YAPS establishes objectives that focus on providing professional experiences to students to improve employability and set them ready for the workforce. We strive to create the best opportunity to develop students' problem-solving, teamwork, time-management, presentation, and research skills.


The Young Australian Problem Solver

Mission

We are highly committed in delivering social impacts and significant contributions to the society by developing students’ employability skills and solving social enterprises’ toughest problems.

Vision

We collaborate students with social enterprises. We strive to create the best real-life experience for student, and expose them to real-world situations. We are here to make a difference and we believe, together with every committed individual, we can establish positive changes in the society.

Our Program

Case for Learning, Achieving, and Progress (CLAP) Semester 2, 2019 Program is an initiative by YAPS to bring global minds from leading Universities in Australia together to solve the toughest problem of social enterprises.

The program involves training by industry experts in management consulting and other professional mentors in related fields via webinars for easy accessibility.

The application deadline for CLAP Semester 2, 2019 is June 9.
