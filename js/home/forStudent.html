For Student

If you are a highly motivated student who wants to be challenged to make a change in the society, as well as getting the first-hand experience and trainings that you need to boost up your employability skills, then you certainly are currently looking at the right program!

Benefits for Students

1. Training & Experience

Get yourself hands-on training and experience into the field of Management Consultancy right from the industry experts with access from any part of the world.

2. Creating Social Impact

Believing in initiating a change and giving back to the society, we extend valuable support in the growth & development of social enterprises.

3. Leadership Development

Developing future leaders by providing them a platform to work on idea generation and problem-solving skills from any corner of the world.

Eligibility

All students, including international students, from any degree of these Universities are more than welcome to apply:

Melbourne

  • University of Melbourne
  • Monash University
  • Royal Melbourne Institute of Technology (RMIT)

Sydney

  • University of New South Wales (UNSW)
  • University of Sydney (USyd)
  • University of Technology Sydney (UTS)

Application process

  1. Fill in this application form before November 18, 2018 to join our CLAP 2018/2019 Summer Program

  2. You would receive an E-Mail confirmation of your successful registration with all the important dates, information as well as the Non-Disclosure Agreement to be signed.

  3. Once signed and sent back, you will be placed in a group by our Human Resources committees and you would receive your Case file.

  4. Attend the webinar on the given date. These webinars will have industry experts guiding you on how to plan your approach to the case and provide you with useful tips to ace the case.

  5. Present your findings and solutions to our panel of judges.

  6. All participants will receive an E-Certificate of Participation and an Award and the opportunity to present to the CEO of our case partner will be given to the team who suggested the most practical solutions.
