For Social Enterprises

If you are a social enterprise who wants to get your problem solve and also committed in giving the opportunity for students to solve your hardest problem, then you are in the perfect place!

Benefits for Social Enterprises

1. Consultancy on your current biggest problems

Connecting you to brilliant and creative minds across the globe who are currently studying in Australia’s leading Universities to ensure you get the best possible solutions and brightest ideas.

2. Support & Guidance

All-round support and guidance in the fields of management consultancy ensuring optimum and efficient results.

3. Social Reach

Allowing you to reach out and connect with young minds from six top Universities in Australia, developing a favourable opinion and brand building.

Application process

  1. Please send us an email to apply for the CLAP Semester 2, 2019 Program.

  2. Our highly committed case team committees will access your application and should you be selected, we will write your case study and send it back to you for confirmation.

  3. Sit back and relax as your problems are solved!
