CLAP Program Overview

1. Students will work in a team to make changes in the society by solving business problems of a social enterprise

2. Students will be equipped with problem solving skills through webinars by professional consultants from top tier consulting firms

3. To conclude the program, students will present their well-researched solutions to our panel of judges and receive valuable feedback on their performance.


Applications Now Open

Applications for Semester 2 Consultants for CLAP Program 2019 at The Young Australian Problem Solver (YAPS) are now open!

YAPS is an independent not-for-profit student-led organisation that aims to enhance students problem-solving skills and helps to solve the social enterprise’s problem.

As a consultant you will:

  • Develop problem-solving skills by working on a real-life case of a Social Enterprise.
  • Learn the art of time-management as well as presentation and research skills.
  • Have the opportunity to be trained by professionals from top tier consulting firms.
  • Further communicability skills through working with a diverse team of like-minded students from different Universities and education backgrounds

Students from ANY educational background are welcome to apply for this opportunity. Prior experience with consulting is appreciated but is NOT COMPULSORY.

Please fill in the application form here to apply.

Application deadline - Sunday, 9th June 2019.

We will review applications on a rolling basis, so the sooner you apply the better!
